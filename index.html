Frequently Asked Questions

What is phishing? +

Phishing is a type of online scam where attackers try to trick you into providing sensitive information like usernames, passwords, and credit card details by pretending to be a trustworthy entity.

How can I recognize a phishing email? +

Phishing emails often have urgent messages, suspicious links, and requests for personal information. Look for poor grammar, spelling errors, and mismatched URLs to identify them.

What should I do if I receive a phishing email? +

If you receive a phishing email, do not click on any links or provide any information. Report the email to your email provider and delete it immediately.

How does your tool detect phishing? +

Our tool uses a combination of URL analysis, email content scanning, and a constantly updated database of known phishing threats to detect and alert you to potential phishing attempts.
