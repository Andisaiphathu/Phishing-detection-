Frequently Asked Questions

+ What are phishing websites?

Phishing websites are fraudulent sites designed to deceive users into providing sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, or credit card details. They often mimic legitimate sites to trick users into believing they are on a trusted platform.

+ How do the Netcraft apps and extensions protect me from phishing attacks?

Netcraft apps and extensions protect users by identifying and blocking phishing sites, scanning emails for suspicious content, and providing real-time alerts. They help to ensure that your online activities are secure by preventing access to malicious websites.

+ What happens when I report a suspicious site?

When you report a suspicious site, the Netcraft team investigates the report and updates their database to protect other users. Your report helps improve the accuracy of phishing detection and contributes to a safer online environment.

+ Which browsers support Netcraft Extension?

Netcraft extensions are supported by major browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Opera. They are designed to integrate seamlessly with these browsers to provide robust phishing protection.

+ Which third-party apps are supported by the Netcraft App?

The Netcraft app supports integration with a variety of third-party apps, including popular email clients and productivity tools. This allows for comprehensive phishing protection across different platforms and services.
